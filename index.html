About Us



Ticket Dada is an aggregator service for the entertainment industry.

Ticketdada.com lets you book movie tickets in your favourite cinema in the simplest, easiest and the smartest way without the unnecessary glitz. Search, Choose & Book movie tickets.

We provide information and assistance with movie tickets booking and tickets of various other events across many cities all over India.

We partner with market-leading companies in the Cinema field with proven business models to provide a smooth ticket booking experience.
